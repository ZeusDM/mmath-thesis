\begin{center}\textbf{Author's Declaration}\end{center}

\noindent
This thesis consists of material all of which I authored or co-authored:
\Cref{chap:introduction,chap:polynomials,chap:models} are my own work;
\Cref{chap:bijective} is based on joint work with my supervisor, Olya Mandelshtam, to appear in an upcoming publication;
